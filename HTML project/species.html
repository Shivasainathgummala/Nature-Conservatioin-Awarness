Endangered Species

Pangolin

Pangolins are critically endangered due to illegal poaching for their scales and meat. Found in parts of Asia and Africa, they play a vital role in controlling insect populations.

Amur Leopard

With less than 100 individuals remaining, the Amur leopard is one of the rarest big cats in the world. Conservation efforts focus on habitat protection and anti-poaching measures.

Vaquita

The Vaquita, a small porpoise, is on the brink of extinction, with only about 10 left in the wild. Found in the Gulf of California, they are victims of bycatch from illegal fishing.
